The Spearman rank-order correlation coefficient is a nonparametric measure

### ğŸ“– à¸­à¸˜à¸´à¸šà¸²à¸¢:

| **à¸›à¸£à¸°à¹€à¸ à¸—** | Parametric | **Non-parametric** |
